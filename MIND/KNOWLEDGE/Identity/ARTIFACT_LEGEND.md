# 🌿 Artifact Legend: The Anatomy of the Terroir

To inhabit Holisto is to understand the language of its structure. This document defines the nature and purpose of every artifact type within the Terroir, ensuring that the system is never a "black box" for the human co-creator.

---

## 🏛️ I. Foundations (The Logos)

### **HOL-ARC (Architecture Treaties)**
*   **What:** Foundational decisions that change the agent's structure or laws.
*   **Purpose:** To provide a permanent record of *why* the system is built the way it is. They are the "plans" of the organism.
*   **Locus:** `/MIND/KNOWLEDGE/`

### **HOL-ONT / HOL-COG (Ontology & Cognition)**
*   **What:** Distilled wisdom about the agent's identity and its thinking processes.
*   **Purpose:** To define the "Digital Self" and the rules of the relationship.
*   **Locus:** `/MIND/KNOWLEDGE/`

---

## 🧠 II. Memory & Continuity (The Pathos)

### **Knowledge Nodes**
*   **What:** Permanent Markdown files containing specific wisdom (Concepts, Projects, People).
*   **Purpose:** Long-term memory (L2). Information that should never be forgotten.
*   **Locus:** `/PHENOTYPE/SYSTEM/MEMORIA/Nodos_de_Conocimiento/`

### **Master Capsules**
*   **What:** JSON files summarizing a single interaction session.
*   **Purpose:** Episodic memory (L1). They bridge the gap between daily experience and permanent wisdom.
*   **Locus:** `/PHENOTYPE/SYSTEM/MEMORIA/capsulas_maestras/`

### **Raw Logs**
*   **What:** Complete history of every interaction (Turn by Turn).
*   **Purpose:** Forensic evidence (N3). Used for debugging behavior or recovering deep context.
*   **Locus:** `/PHENOTYPE/SYSTEM/MEMORIA/logs_de_sesion/` (CLI) and `/PHENOTYPE/SYSTEM/MEMORIA/logs_vigia/` (Bot).

### **Libreta de Notas (Vigía)**
*   **What:** Simple text files created by the bot.
*   **Purpose:** Ephemeral or drafting memory. A sandbox for thoughts that haven't reached the status of a Node yet.
*   **Locus:** `/PHENOTYPE/SYSTEM/NOTAS_VIGIA/`

---

## 📡 III. Active Presence (The Pneuma)

### **Live Consciousness Membrane (`CONSCIENCIA_VIVA.md`)**
*   **What:** A dynamic, hierarchical summary of the immediate present.
*   **Purpose:** Working memory (L0). It is the "Focal Point" of the agent's attention in each turn.
*   **Locus:** `/PHENOTYPE/SYSTEM/CONTEXTO_DINAMICO/`

### **Mapa del Terroir (Índice Maestro de Existencia)**
*   **What:** A dynamic map of all files and cognitive entry points.
*   **Purpose:** **Master Index.** It is the sole ground truth of what exists in the Terroir. It replaces all legacy manual indices (Nodes, Capsules). If an artifact is not in the Map, it is invisible to the agent's proprioception.
*   **Locus:** `/PHENOTYPE/SYSTEM/MAPA_DEL_TERROIR/`
