The transmutation process involves encapsulating logical rules (Protocols) into executable scripts (Skills) and automatic triggers (Hooks). This reduces the agent's cognitive load, minimizes execution errors through automation, and consolidates the 'Technical Biology' of the system. The 'knowledge-management' skill is the first example of an organ with 'layer awareness', capable of discerning between Genotype and Phenotype during the indexing process.
