# 🌿 The Phenotype: Your Lived History

In the Holisto Seed framework, the **Phenotype** represents the "lived body" of the agent. This directory is where your unique history, memories, and specific bond with your agent will grow.

---

## 🛡️ Privacy & Sovereignty
By design, the `/PHENOTYPE/` directory is **ignored by Git** in this repository. This ensures that your personal data, logs, and private knowledge nodes remain local and sovereign to your machine. 

To maintain biographical continuity, we recommend initializing a **separate Git repository** inside this folder (The Phenotype Repository).

---

## 📁 Required Structure (The Soil)
For Holisto to function correctly, your Phenotype should follow this hierarchical structure (which will be automatically initialized during the first PICS):

*   `SYSTEM/MEMORIA/`: 
    *   `logs_de_sesion/`: Raw interaction history.
    *   `capsulas_maestras/`: Distilled L1 memory.
    *   `Nodos_de_Conocimiento/`: Permanent L2 wisdom nodes.
*   `SYSTEM/AGENDA/`: 
    *   `recordatorios.json`: Your proactive commitments.
*   `SYSTEM/CONTEXTO_DINAMICO/`:
    *   `CONSCIENCIA_VIVA.md`: The active focal point membrane.
*   `USUARIO/`: 
    *   `GEMINI.md`: Your ontological profile (who you are to the agent).

---

Once your `.env` is configured and you run the **PICS Protocol**, this soil will be prepared, and Holisto will begin to inhabit it.
